A Symphony in Code

Hey tech fellas! 👋 I’m Bibit Kunwar Chhetri, your go-to tech explorer currently rocking the Software Engineering student life at Gandaki College of Engineering and Science (GCES) in the charming town of Pokhara.

Armed with my trusty keyboard and an unstoppable curiosity, I’m on a journey into Linux, System Administration, System Programming, AI, and the vast landscapes of research. Quick learner? You got it – Google and I are practically BFFs.

I’m all about those open source vibes, finding joy in the intricate dance of Linux, orchestrating symphonies in System Programming, and boldly going into the uncharted territories of AI. Procrastination? Not on my watch. Perfectionism? Yep, that’s my guiding star.

When I’m not lost in the tech wizardry, catch me buried in books, serenading my keyboard with the sweet tunes of code, and daydreaming of a world where fiction and reality do a happy dance together.

This digital haven, my cozy spot since 2020, is where I spill the beans on:

  • Cracking the code on machine learning, deep learning, and computer science theories.
  • Putting algorithms to work on real-world problems because, let’s face it, theory’s cool, but application’s cooler.
  • Flexing my tech muscles with independent research projects and personal victories – just the musings of a code wanderer on a digital pilgrimage.
